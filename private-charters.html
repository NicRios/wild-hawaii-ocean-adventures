About This Tour

An Unforgettable Ocean Experience

Have a special event and want to reserve the Ocean Warrior for a private charter? Give us a call. We're happy to host birthday parties, honeymoon outings, photo shoots, bachelor and bachelorette parties, private getaways, and many other special occasions through custom charters.

For more information on a private charter, give us a call at 808-854-4401 or email info@wild-hawaii.com and we'll help you plan the perfect day on the water.

Cancellation policy: private charters require 72 hours' advance notice for cancellation.

What's Included

  • All snorkel equipment (mask, fins, snorkel, wetsuit top)
  • Flotation devices and safety briefing
  • Light snacks and bottled water
  • Expert naturalist guide
  • Complimentary reef-safe sunscreen
  • Dry bags for your valuables

What to Bring

  • Swimsuit worn under your clothing
  • Sunglasses and a hat
  • Motion sickness medication if needed
  • Towel and change of clothes
  • Camera or GoPro
  • Cash or card for tips and photo purchases

Meet Spot Directions

How To Get Here

Search Wild Hawaii Ocean Adventures in Google Maps, Apple Maps, or Waze — it will lead you right to our meeting spot. Do not enter the marina street address; it will take you to the wrong location.

Honokohau Marina & Small Boat Harbor
Kealakehe Pkwy, Kailua-Kona, HI 96740

  1. From Hwy 19, turn WEST at Kealakehe Pkwy at the "Honokohau Marina and Small Boat Harbor" sign.
  2. Take the first RIGHT toward the NORTH side of the harbor, passing Gentry's and the gas station on your left.
  3. Meet us between the 2 boat ramps in the parking lot by the public restrooms.
