About Us

We are Wild Hawaii Ocean Adventures

We are uniquely capable of providing an ocean experience like no other company in Kona — and we do it through a combination of our equipment and our talented crew.

At the heart of every trip is the Ocean Warrior, an 11-meter rigid-hull inflatable built for Naval Special Warfare. Twin 500 HP Caterpillar engines and KAMEWA Rolls-Royce jet drives make it blisteringly fast and remarkably maneuverable — and because jet drives have no exposed propellers, it's an exceptionally safe dive platform for both marine life and swimmers. Dubbed the "Lamborghini" of the fleet, the Ocean Warrior served with Special Boat Team 12 before bringing ocean lovers to remote spots other boats simply can't reach.

But equipment alone doesn't make a great experience — a knowledgeable, seasoned crew does. Wild Hawaii Ocean Adventures runs with a team that has spent their lives on the waters off the Kona coast: championship freedivers, professional underwater photographers, expert pelagic guides, and seasoned captains.
