Answers Before You Even Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on a tour?
Bring a swimsuit worn under your clothes, a towel, sunglasses, a hat, reef-safe sunscreen, and a camera or GoPro. We provide all snorkel gear, flotation devices, water, and light snacks. Bring a little cash for tips and optional photo purchases.
Are tours appropriate for children?
Yes — most of our tours welcome all ages and skill levels. Children must be comfortable in the water and accompanied by an adult. The Manta Night Snorkel is best for confident young swimmers; reach out and we'll recommend the right fit.
Do I need prior snorkeling or swimming experience?
No experience is required for most tours. Our guides give a full safety briefing and provide flotation devices, so even first-timers are comfortable. All skill levels are welcome on our reef and marine-life tours.
What marine life can I expect to see?
Depending on the tour and season: green sea turtles, spinner dolphins, reef and pelagic fish, manta rays, eels, octopus, and — from December to March — humpback whales. Every encounter is wild, so sightings are never guaranteed, but they're frequent.
Is snorkel equipment included?
Yes. Mask, snorkel, fins, and wetsuit tops are all included, along with flotation devices and a safety briefing. Underwater photos are available for purchase on most tours.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least a few days ahead, and earlier during peak season (winter whale season and holidays). Popular tours like the Manta Night Snorkel fill quickly. Spots are limited and groups stay small.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
We require 24 hours' advance notice for a full refund on standard tours (72 hours for private charters). Tours cancelled by us for weather or safety are fully refunded or rescheduled at no charge.
What happens if the weather is poor?
Your safety comes first. If conditions aren't right, we'll relocate to a calmer spot when possible or reschedule your tour. If we cancel, you'll receive a full refund or a free rebooking — no questions asked.
