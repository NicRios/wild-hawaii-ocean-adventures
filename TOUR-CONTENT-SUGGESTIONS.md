current pages use the placeholder prices/times from the Figma design. The live

important ways. Apply these everywhere a tour appears: the homepage cards, the

## The most important changes

1. **Add "observer / non-swimmer" pricing.** This is a real differentiator the
   current site is missing. Every snorkel tour offers a reduced rate for people
   who come along but don't get in the water (Captain Cook $90, Coastal $100,
   Manta $90, Offshore $130). Surface it on the quick-facts bar and booking card,
   e.g. a second price line: *"$90 / person for observers."*

2. **Fix the start times — two tours are afternoon, not morning.**
   - Captain Cook Snorkel is an **afternoon** tour (2:00–4:30 PM). The current
     itinerary starts at 7:00 AM — that's wrong; rebuild it around a ~2 PM check-in.
   - Whale Watching is **afternoon** (2:00–4:00 PM), not 8 AM.
   - Coastal and Offshore are **9:00 AM**; Manta is **5:30 PM** (already evening ✓).

3. **Lower the prices** to match the live site (see table). All are $10–15 lower
   except Whale Watching (unchanged).

4. **Tighten group sizes** to the real "4–N" ranges (note the **minimum of 4**),
   especially Offshore (**4–8**, not "up to 11") and Captain Cook (**4–11**, not
   "up to 14").

5. **Private Charters is quote-based.** Drop the "$175/person" and the fixed
   "4 Hrs / up to 11" facts. Replace with: *"Half- or full-day charters, no
   minimum passengers — call 808-854-4401 or email for a custom quote."*
